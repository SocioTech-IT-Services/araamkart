Shipping policy

We aim to deliver wholesale orders within Shillong as per agreed slots and route planning. Delivery may depend on product availability, weather, and order volume. Any fee or minimum order rules are shown at checkout or communicated by our team before dispatch.

Bulk returns & refunds

Damaged, incorrect, or short-supplied items should be reported within 48 hours of delivery with photos and invoice reference. Eligible cases may be replaced or adjusted as credit, subject to verification. Perishable or opened goods may not be eligible unless defective.

B2B terms of use

By registering and placing orders you confirm you are a business / trade buyer. Prices, packs, and availability may change. You are responsible for accurate GST and billing details. Misuse of accounts, fraudulent orders, or resale violations may lead to suspension.

Privacy policy

We collect contact and order data needed to fulfil deliveries and support your account. We do not sell personal data. Messages (email/WhatsApp) may be used for order updates and service notices. You may request corrections or raise concerns via our contact details.
